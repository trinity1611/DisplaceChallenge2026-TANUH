Mastering the Platform

Turn raw medical audio into structured clinical intelligence in 4 simple steps.

1. Prepare Audio

Ensure your audio is .wav (16kHz), at least 10 minutes long, and under 25 MB.

2. Select Language

Pick the primary language spoken in your recording (Hindi or Kannada) from the top dropdown.

3. Upload & Process

Upload the file and hit Process. Grab a coffee—AI analysis takes 7-10 minutes. Do not refresh!

4. Export Insights

Review the AI-generated clinical summary and transcript. Copy text or download the text file.
